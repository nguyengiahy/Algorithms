Given a RPN (Reverse Polish Notation) a.k.a postfix notation. Calculate the result of that RPN.

Input: A string has less than 255 characters, contains numbers and 4 operations (+, -, *, /). Any two adjacent 
numbers are seperated by a space.
Output: Result of that RPN.

Example:
Input: 10 2/3 + 7 4 -*
Output: 24
