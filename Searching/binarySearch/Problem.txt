Search for a given element in a sorted array.

Input:  number of array elements, n.
	n numbers for array elements.
	search value.
Output: -1 if search value doesn't exist in the array,
	else the index of that value in the array.

Example:
Input: 	6
	4 8 10 7 5 3
	5
Output: Sorted array:
	3 4 5 7 8 10
	2

Example 2:
Input: 	6
	4 8 10 7 5 3
	9
Output: Sorted array:
	3 4 5 7 8 10
	-1
